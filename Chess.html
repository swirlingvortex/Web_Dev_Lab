Chess is a very fun game where the goal of the game is to Checkmate the enemy king.
In chess there are 6 different pieces and each piece moves differently. The pieces are the

  • King
  • Queen
  • Rook
  • Bishop
  • Knight
  • Pawn

Check is when the king is being attacked, and Checkmate is when the king is being attacked,
and cannot avoid capture on the next turn.
